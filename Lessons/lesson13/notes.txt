BIG O


math version
f(n) is O(g(n))
if there exists
  c, no
such that
cg(n)>= f(n) n>no


Ex:
f(n) = 1000n
g(n) = 1/10*n^2

Q: Is f(n) O(g(n)) | is 1000n(1/10*n^2)

c = 1
n0 = 10^6

f = 10^9
g = 10^11
